About the Movie

A quiet, emotionally charged drama about Uma, a radio presenter who has raised her son Ninad alone. As Ninad pursues his ambitious career, distance grows between them until an unexpected project brings them back into each other's orbit. What begins as a routine task gradually uncovers buried warmth, unspoken care, and the fragile threads that hold a family together.
